Examples for RouteTracking.js

Example 1: A route with one source, three intermediate stations, and one destination

Hover over your mouse on any station to see details of stations in order on route on the right.

Note: the upper-left corner is (x = 0, y = 0)

Current Mouse Position: (x = NaN, y = NaN)

Example 2: The same setting as before.

Double Click near the line(path) to add a "current location" or double click anywhere to add an intermediate station.

Note: there can be only one current location.
The intermediate location will be added as the last intermediate station before destination

Try to drag and drop any station to change its position.

Double Click any intermediate station or current location to interchange their types.

Current Mouse Position: (x = NaN, y = NaN)
